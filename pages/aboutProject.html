About Project

This is simple weather app project with the help of HTML Javascript, CSS and Open Weather API.

The feature of this project is listed below :

  1. You can see the temperature of your city.
  2. You can see the humidity of your city.
  3. You can see the air pressure of your city and also see wind speed.

The upcoming feature of this project is listed below :

  1. You can see the minimum and maximum temperature of your city along with the sunrise and sunset times.
  2. you can see longitude and latitude of you city and also see sea lavel.
